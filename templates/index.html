Frequently Asked Questions

How do I chat and learn a language with one of my friends?

Go to the search bar on the top of the page, type in your friends username, and press submit.

Am I able to access my previous chats?

Yes! Navigate to the 'Your Chats' button on the top of the page and once you click on the page, you will be able to access previous chats under the 'Go to Chatroom' column, you will be able to access previous chats.

How many languages can I be proficient in?

As of right now, each user is able to be proficient in one language at a time.

How do I start learning another language?

Just enter in a language that you're hoping to learn in the search bar, and you will be matched with another user who is seeking to learn the language you are proficient at, while also being able to teach you the language that you want to learn.

Am I able to have friends on this platform?

Of course; we are trying to foster relationships between bilinguals! All users that you have previously chatted with, are automatically added to your friends list.

Where can I find my friends that I have previous chatted with?

Once you navigate to the dashboard page once you are logged in, your friends list will appear on the bottom of the page.
